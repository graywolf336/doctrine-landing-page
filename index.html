Incarnation Seminar

Apostolic Seminar on the Incarnation
Was located in Kansas City, KS on September 9th and 10th

About the Seminar

It was held on September 9th from 7pm to 10pm and then the following day, September 10th from 8am to 12pm (noon).

The seminar's topic was the Incarnation, where Apostolic ministers presented papers concerning the topic.

Todd Nance, Mike Conn, and William Chalfant are the sponsors.
